Some friendly advice

Learning a language is a tough process. You will have to do it piece by piece. Don't try to memorize too many new things a day because that will exhaust you quickly. Here's a few things to try to keep in mind while learning.
  • Don't memorize too much everyday
  • Try to practise everyday. Even if its only 5 minutes, thats better that nothing
  • Try to find the motivation behind wanting to learn a language and work towards that.
  • Lastly, remember to have fun with your studies!
